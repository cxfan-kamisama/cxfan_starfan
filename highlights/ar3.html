About GPA Calculation in NJU

In Nanjing University, Grade Point Average (GPA) is calculated by doing a weighted average, that is, summing up final mark of all courses multiplied by credit, and then divided by total credit. To scale from 0 to 5, the result should finally be divided by 20. The formula is as follows:
